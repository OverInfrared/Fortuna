Fortuna

Fortuna generates entirely new ores, materials, tools, and armor from a single seed. Every seed produces a unique set of resources with their own names, colors, textures, mining levels, and world generation patterns. What you find depends on the luck of the draw.
Crafting recipes, smelting, armor trims, loot tables, and ore distribution are all generated at runtime and injected seamlessly into the game through dynamic data and resource packs. 

No two seeds play the same.
Fortuna ensures every world has its own riches to discover.

Built for Minecraft 1.21.1 on Fabric.
